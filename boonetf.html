Portfolio allocation

Your portfolio is composed of 0 to 100% depending on the individually selected equity ratio. This means that the portfolio can range from one that is entirely made up of corporate and government bonds to one that is purely invested in equities. With the latter, you are most exposed to the fluctuations on the global stock markets. As a premium for this risk, higher returns can be expected in the long term than with an investment with a bond component.
